So, I (Rafael) was working on some tech to help me develop games faster such as a general purpose physics editor, and I was working on another secret project (that I might show to the public later this year), when I saw someone talking about an event that was about to happen in a month here in Brazil: the SBGames 2014.

"What is this?", you might ask. It's an event held annually here in Brazil which started in 2002 and helps propagate the game culture in my country. Anyway, I saw an oportunity there, but only a month. Can I do it? I'll be sharing my thoughts through this sequences of posts.

 

Well, we have to start somewhere (it has to start sometime. What better place than here? What better time than now?). We needed to think of a game, instead of coming with something entirely new, maybe I should make Silver Lining in the way I was envisioning in the Global Game Jam (which unfortunately can't even be a game :/)
